What is multilevel marketing ?

Multilevel marketing (MLM) is a business model where a affiliates to sell products or services.
Affiliates earn commissions not only for their own sales but also for recruiting new affiliates into their downline.
This creates a hierarchical structure with multiple levels, where higher-level affiliates receive a portion of the earnings generated by those they recruit.
